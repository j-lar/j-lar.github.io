Letter to Blockbuster™

I didn’t watch Titanic.
Perhaps it should be noted
that I also have not been involved
in a serious relationship since
approximately the date of its release,
and the last girl who showed any
promise and interest left the country
soon after I was forced to admit
the truth in a particularly ruthless
round of truth or dare.
This requires investigation.
For the gods of fate to tempt me
by means of movies about
fate is quite unnecessary.
It’s not even ironic. I want a refund.
It shouldn’t end like this.
