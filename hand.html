My hand betrays me like a sparrow
Searching for God. There is a story
About a girl who danced when she moved
Her feet and felt like God would not
Approve of such an all consuming
Trait. She made herself devout
And still until, by virtue of her
Deprivation she died young and
Went to heaven like they say
In books and funerals. Senseless,
My fingers write to recover thoughts,
Seeking causes where there are none.
