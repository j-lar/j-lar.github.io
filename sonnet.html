Sonnet

Tan fo clara ma prima lutz d'eslir
lei don cre'l cors los huelhs.
                               -Daniel Arnaut

The brightness of the eyes that made me choose,
or was the brightness of enlightenment,
my heart believing my eyes, what I meant?
Like a translation coming, slow and loose,
to one who struggles with the words to use,
I find the language imprecise, and hint
at what I mean. The eyes are what I want,
the eyes, and perfect curve of brow, of her whose
eyes my heart fears, and my eyes, eyes my heart
believes this time. So clear it was, that flash
of choosing her, ma prima lutz d'eslir,
that even now I see los huelhs vert
and my heart shivers with the thought of lashes
that close over the eyes that my heart fears.
