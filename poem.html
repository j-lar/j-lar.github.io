Poem

I haven’t seen you for a week
And even then you weren’t really
There. No, not for a long time,
Not since my mind was full

Of possibilities and you
Were too. Now, I can’t tell what you
Are full of and my mind is empty,
Blank as a page in this notebook,
As blank as ten pages, probably

More. If I can’t find you where will
I be ten years from now, still looking?
Or waiting for inspiration
To strike, like lighting, the same place twice.
